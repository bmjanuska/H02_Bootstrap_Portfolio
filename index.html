About Me

My name is Bernadette, but most call me Bernie. I am a graphic designer and illustrator with junior experience in front end web development. Advanced user of the Adobe Creative Suite, and familiar with HTML, CSS, Javascript, and Wordpress. Experienced with print and print production processes as well. In addition to my technical skill set, I am experienced in communicating ideas to a wide variety of audiences, collaborating with different types of teams across a company, and always welcome feedback from peers and clients alike. Love to draw little comics and doodles of daily cuteness.
